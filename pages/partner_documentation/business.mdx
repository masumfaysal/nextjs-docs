## Business Information
Business information means the business name, business address, business telephone number, and name of the owner, officer, and director; job titles; and many more.

## Address

A business address is the official location of a company's premises. It could be anything from someone's home address right up to a multi-million-pound campus, such as those big tech companies' preferred addresses, and everything in between.
